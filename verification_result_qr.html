Frequently Asked Questions

How long is this verification valid?

Your SkyCheck verification is valid for 24 hours from the time of issue, or until your scheduled flight departure, whichever comes first.

Can I use this for international flights?

Yes, SkyCheck is integrated with over 150 international carriers and regional security protocols for seamless cross-border travel.

What if my flight details change?

If your airline updates your flight number or time, simply re-scan your latest digital boarding pass to sync the verification data.
